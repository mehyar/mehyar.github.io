Mehyar Science Foundation

As a doctor she helped numerous patients, and as a mother she dedicated her life to raising her children.
If I have any success in my life, it's indebted to her.

This foundation aims to make charitable contributions to:

- Science and engineering education
- Physical health and medical research
- Childhood/Youth education, teaching, and learning
